Installation:
-------------

1) Copy the Birds3D folder to your ~/.nuke folder.

2) Open your ~/.nuke/init.py in a text editor and add the following line:

nuke.pluginAddPath('./Birds3D')


3) Save and close your init.py. When you open Nuke, you should see Birds3D in your toolbar.



~/.nuke folder default locations:

Windows: C:/Users/-YOUR USERNAME-/.nuke
Linux:   /home/-YOUR USERNAME-/.nuke
Mac OS:  /Users/-YOUR USERNAME-/.nuke
